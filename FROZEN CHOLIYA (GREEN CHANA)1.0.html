FROZEN CHOLIYA (GREEN CHANA)

APPEARANCE & SIZE: Green color with a maximum diameter of 12 mm.
SEASONAL AVAILABILITY: December to March
  • DESCRIPTION
  • NUTRITION

Fresh green Choliya is de-podded manually at the farm level at very well-maintained collection centers where importance is given to quality and hygiene. The green Choliya is then brought to the processing center where it is float washed, blanched, chilled and frozen in an IQF to get individually frozen Choliya. These are then weighed, packed and stored in frozen chambers of the cold storage at -18 deg C.
