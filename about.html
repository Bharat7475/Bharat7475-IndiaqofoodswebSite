About

We make frozen food the world loves

IndiaQo Foods Pvt Ltd supplies premium frozen sweet corn and diced vegetables year-round to retail, foodservice, and industrial clients. We prioritize exceptional quality and guaranteed food safety, sourcing the finest produce from top growers. Our product lines offer a variety of pack styles, including whole and sliced IQF sweet corn and diced vegetables, available under our label or private label. With top-of-the-line packaging options, we ensure fast freezing for optimal quality in every batch.

Mission Statement

To process and promote tasty, healthy, hygienic and nutritious frozen foods for better health and wellbeing for all, now and for future generations.

Vision Statement

To be the world’s most successful and important frozen food company with sustainable and environment friendly practices providing wholesome frozen foods with outstanding quality and value.

Company Philosophy

Focus on Quality and Values. Imbibe Discipline & Empathy
